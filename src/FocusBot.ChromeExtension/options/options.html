FocusBot Companion

Browser extension settings

Connection

Desktop App Checking...

The extension sends browser context to the FocusBot desktop app running on localhost:51789.

Tracking

When enabled, the extension sends active tab info (URL, domain, title) to the desktop app for focus classification.

Temporarily stop tracking for a set duration.

Overlay

Displays a small indicator in the corner of your browser showing your current focus state.

Privacy

What is collected

  • Active tab URL and domain
  • Page title
  • Tab/window activity timing

What is NOT collected

  • Page content or DOM
  • Typed input or form data
  • Passwords
  • Screenshots
  • Incognito tab data

Excluded Domains

Domains listed below are never tracked. Banking and internal browser pages are excluded by default.

All data is stored locally and sent only to the desktop app on localhost. Nothing is sent to the cloud.
